Terms & Conditions

Last Updated: July 2026

Welcome to CSC AP Tour & Travels. These terms and conditions outline the rules and regulations for the use of our centralized portal.

By accessing this website, we assume you accept these terms and conditions in full.

1. Use of Services (Travel & e-Stamping)

This portal is specifically designed to assist Common Service Centre (CSC) Village Level Entrepreneurs (VLEs) in Andhra Pradesh. All bookings (IRCTC, Redbus, Airways) and e-Stamping requests must be made following the official guidelines provided by CSC SPV.

2. Accuracy of Materials

Users are responsible for providing accurate and truthful information while filling out passenger details for ticket bookings. CSC AP Tour & Travels does not warrant that any of the materials on its website are entirely error-free.

3. Limitation of Liability

In no event shall CSC AP Tour & Travels or its developers be held liable for any damages, ticket booking failures, or monetary loss arising out of server downtimes from IRCTC or other API partners.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2026

At CSC AP Tour & Travels, we prioritize the privacy of our VLEs and citizens. This document contains types of information that is collected and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our portal for Travel or e-Stamping services, we may collect:

  • Contact Information: Mobile Number, Email Address.
  • Official Identifiers: [Aadhaar Redacted], PAN Card Number, CSC ID Number.
  • Passenger Data: Details required strictly for ticket booking purposes.
2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information to process and manage your ticket bookings, provide technical support, and prevent fraudulent activities. We do not sell or rent your data to any third-party marketing agencies.

3. Data Security

We strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting personal information. Data transmitted during bookings is secured via official APIs.

Disclaimer

The information and booking interfaces provided by CSC AP Tour & Travels are for authorized VLE use only.

1. External Links & API Dependencies

Our portal acts as a bridge connecting VLEs to primary booking platforms (IRCTC, Redbus, MakeMyTrip, APSRTC). We do not guarantee the uptime of these third-party servers.

2. Booking & Refund Policy

All ticket cancellations and refunds are subject to the policies of the respective travel partner (e.g., Indian Railways). We encourage VLEs to consult the official support of the respective merchant for delayed refunds.

3. Use at Your Own Risk

All software and reporting tools provided on this site are for the convenience of VLEs. Use of this portal during critical booking hours is solely at your own risk.
